Risk Protections
⚡
Break-even — once price rises to the trigger %, the stop loss moves to entry so the position can never close at a loss.
🔻
Trailing stop — stop loss follows the price upward, locking in gains as the trade runs in our favour.
🛑
Fixed stop — standard stop loss at a fixed % below entry price.
Entry Filters
📉
Regime filter — blocks new BUY entries when ADX is below the threshold, indicating a ranging/choppy market with no clear trend.
🔗
Correlation filter — skips a BUY if an open position is already highly correlated with the incoming coin (Pearson r), avoiding doubling up on the same market move.
